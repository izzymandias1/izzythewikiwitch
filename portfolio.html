the sojourn

teldrani

i created teldrani for the sojourn audio drama.
teldrani is a conlang primarily based on japanese, with sprinkles of arabic and welsh in there too (because i hate you and myself).
it has been included in things such as a lore short and the audio drama itself. many actors have suffered it and i thank them from all from the bottom of my heart.
